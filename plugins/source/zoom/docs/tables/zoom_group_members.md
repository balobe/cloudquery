| ------------- | ------------- | -----  |
|group_id|text|Group ID.|
|id|text|Member ID.|
|email|text|Member email.|
|first_name|text|Member first name.|
|last_name|text|Member last name.|
|type|bigint|Member type.|
|department|text|Member department.|
